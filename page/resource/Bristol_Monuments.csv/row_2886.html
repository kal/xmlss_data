Render with limestone dressings, roof not visible. Single rooms front and rear with a central right-hand light well, and stair to the rear of the well. A fairly complete C19 shop front with left-hand door to the flat, right-hand to the shop, with a central plate-glass window. At the rear incorporates elements from the medieval period and later, also in the party wall with No.43 adjacent to the north.
