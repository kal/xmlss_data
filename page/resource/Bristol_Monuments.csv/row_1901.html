monument_desc Bristol Tramways Company Depot located on the northern side of Avonmouth Road, Avonmouth. The building was constructed in 1915 and was probably designed by the Bristol architect W. H. Watkins. The building is a single-storey brick structure with pitched tiled roofs in an Arts and Crafts style. Much of the main, south-west facing elevation comprises a range of sliding timber doors to the garage and the garage entrance piers are rusticated render with alternate brick quoins. At the centre of the elevation and also in the office range there are two steep gables coontaining tympana decorated with plaster cartouches bearing the date 1915. At the south-west corner of the building there is a square corner turret containing the entrance, a semicircular-arched doorway, to the office range on the west side of the building with clock face above.
The building has a Grade II listing (Listed Building number 901-1/52-357).
