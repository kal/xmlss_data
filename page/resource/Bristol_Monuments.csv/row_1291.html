monument_desc Clift Cottage, located on the eastern side of Coronation Road, Bedminster. The house was constructed between 1826 and 1843 at the southern end of a land parcel which had been in use as a nursery in the early twentieth century. The Bedminster tithe survey of 1843 (BRO EP/A/32/8) records the land parcel as a house and garden owned by St. Nicholas Church and occupied by Thomas Menlove. A stable was constructed on the property in 1889 (BRO Building Plans Vol.24 fo.80). The house survived into the early twentieth century but was apparently demolished when a motor garage was constructed in 1920.
