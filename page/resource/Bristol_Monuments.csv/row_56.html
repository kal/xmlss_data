monument_desc The Master's House of the St. Mark's Hospital, also known as Gaunt's Hospital.
By 1913 the building was incorporated into the business premises of Messrs. John Harvey & Sons Ltd., whose family was reported by Pritchard to have been "in possession of those buildings since 1796" (Pritchard 1913, 128). Pritchard also reported that a small window had been discovered "quite recently" and had been opened up. The building had been destroyed by bombing by February 1941 (BUAD 4215).
The site is now part of the post-Second World War offices of Harvey's Wine Merchants.
