monument_desc Army Ordnance Department (A.O.D.) store located approximately 180 metres to the north of Greensplot Farm. The store was built in 1918 to serve National Filling Factory No.23 and is recorded by a plan in the National Archives (MUN 4/1753) dated 27 November 1918. The building measured approximately 32 metres from north to south and was 26 metres wide with a double-pile plan.
The shell of the building was extant in 1946 but the roof had been removed by that date. The remainder of the structure was subsequently demolished.
