monument_desc Cemetery of Bristol Cathedral, located to the east of the chapter house. The burial ground is an inhumation cemetery established to serve St. Augustine's Abbey (BUAD 58M), presumably c1170. It is likely that the majority of the burials associated with the abbey took place in College Green, with this smaller cemetery being reserved for the more senior members of the religious community. After the dissolution of the abbey in 1534 the burial ground was taken over as the cemetery of Bristol Cathedral.
An Order in Council of 18 February 1854 made under the Burial Act of 1852 imposed the restriction that no burials were to take place "within five yards of any house, and only one body [was] to be buried in each grave" in the cemetery.
