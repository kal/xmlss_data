monument_desc The Hope Chapel, of the Methodist New Connexion. The chapel was located on the north side of Castle Green. Designed by H. Crisp, it opened in May 1855 with seating for 800. A schoolroom and vestries were attached (Major, 1872 100).
The building had a two-storey front in the Italian style, five-window range, with a wide central bay of three windows breaking forward. Banded rustication to the ground-floor; string; cornice and short parapet. 3 round-arched openings to centre of ground-floor, the middle taller, leading into a porch; 1 round-arched window to either side. Five round-arched first-floor windows. The frontage was set back from street, with railings to the pavement. It may be presumed to have had a gallery.
