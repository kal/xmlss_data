monument_desc Redhouse Farm. The site of the farm is located on the west side of Hareclive Road immediately to the north of the junction with Gatehouse Avenue. The farm was in existence from at least 1817 (Burchill 1993) and is recorded by the tithe survey for Bedminster (BRO EP/A/32/8). This indicates that at that time the farm comprised a farmhouse and two outbuildings, immediately to the west and north-west. In the 1930s or later the farm buildings were demolished. The site was partly excavated in the early 1990s (SMR 20049) and stone rubble foundations, dated to the eighteenth century by pottery finds, were recorded (Burchill n.d.). The site was subsequently re-developed.
